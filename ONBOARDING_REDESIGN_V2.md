# Onboarding Tutorial - Psychological Marketing Redesign V2

## Marketing Psychology Principles Applied

### Core Psychological Triggers
1. **Loss Aversion** - People fear losing more than they desire gaining
2. **Concrete Specificity** - Specific numbers > vague promises
3. **Time-Based Urgency** - "Right now" creates action
4. **Pain ‚Üí Pleasure Journey** - Acknowledge pain, show transformation
5. **Social Proof** - Others are already winning
6. **Instant Gratification** - Promise immediate results
7. **Identity Reinforcement** - "You're the type of person who..."
